Welcome to R.E.A.C.H

Responsibility to Engineer Care and Humanity

We are a fast growing charity platform in India dedicated to make the Rural and tribal lives
in the corners and depths of the country better. We aim to be their torch in hand as they come into the light of this world.




VISION

To uplift the rural and minority communities in India a to higher social status and
eradicate all the discrimination, poverty and communal difficulties they face.

MISSION

To provide better education, food and other resources for those in need
By which they will find the strength to stand on their feet again.
